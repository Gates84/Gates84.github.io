Project Manager Game

Project Manager is a game that teaches management and leadership. The player is a relatively new "Project Manager‚Äù for a company conducting research for a new mobile application. In-game, you can walk around the office talking to your team, check your email, and speak with your boss in her office.

Your actions and decisions are assessed by two bars displayed in the top left corner. Your decisions have a direct effect on the morale of the team and the progress of the project. Disgruntled team members work slowly and making poor decisions could cost your team time and efficiency.
